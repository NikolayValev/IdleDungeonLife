Idle Dungeon
Life

Every character lives once.

Send your adventurer into ancient dungeons — they age, collect traits, and push deeper until time runs out. When they fall, legacy ash persists across deaths: permanent unlocks, chosen paths, and a world that remembers every run.

Traits & Evolution

Hidden aspects surface as your character ages. Some evolve mid-run, permanently reshaping your build.

Dungeon Depths

15 locations from the Abandoned Chapel to The Wound — each harder, richer, and more lethal.

Life & Death

Your adventurer ages in real time. Death is progress — legacy ash funds everything that follows.

Legacy Paths

Holy, Abyss, or Knowledge. Each path unlocks a talent tree and perk line spanning multiple lives.
